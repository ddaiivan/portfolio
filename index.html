About Me

Experientia docet. Leveraging diverse experiences and a passion for learning to enhance health standards, drive sustainability, and create positive change in Indonesia.

Experientia (Experience): This refers to the knowledge or skill gained through direct observation, participation, or involvement in events or activities. It's not theoretical knowledge from books or lectures, but rather the practical understanding that comes from doing and living. This includes both successes and failures; we learn from both.
Docet (Teaches): This is the verb, indicating that experience is the instructor, the source of learning. It implies a process of gaining wisdom, understanding, and skill.
